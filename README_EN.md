## 🔰 1. Getting Started · Start Here

> **Embodied AI** refers to intelligent systems that perceive, understand, decide, and act through physical interaction with the environment. It integrates computer vision, robotics, reinforcement learning, natural language processing, and other fields, representing a critical path toward general artificial intelligence.

This repository is designed as a **structured resource index + industry knowledge map**, not a tutorial library. Our philosophy: **Find what you need, when you need it**.

## 🧭 2. Learning Path – From Novice to Expert

> If you are a beginner, we recommend the following progressive path:

### 🪜 2.1 Foundation: Build a Knowledge Framework

Start with the **[Foundations](docs/00-basics.md)** section:
- Read recommended books and take courses to understand the fundamentals of robotics, reinforcement learning, and computer vision.
- Master core terminology (Sim2Real, VLA, Imitation Learning, etc.) to prepare for reading research papers.

### 🔭 2.2 Exploration: Track Cutting‑Edge Research

Dive into **[Papers & Code](docs/03-papers-code.md)**:
- Browse the latest top-tier papers by category (VLA, manipulation, navigation, simulation, etc.), and explore accompanying open-source code.
- Review survey papers to quickly grasp the landscape and development trends.

### 🔗 2.3 Connection: Gain Industry Insights

Explore the **[Company List](docs/01-companies.md)** and **[Job Listings](docs/02-jobs.md)**:
- Learn which companies are active in Embodied AI, their core products, and application scenarios.
- Identify the skills most in demand by analyzing job postings, and tailor your learning accordingly.

### 🌐 2.4 Immersion: Join the Community

Engage with the **[Learning Community](docs/05-community.md)**:
- Follow thought leaders on social media and participate in online discussions.
- Contribute to open-source projects or competitions to gain hands-on experience.
